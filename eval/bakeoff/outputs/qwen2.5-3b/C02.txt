 In Nigeria, the exclusive jurisdiction for employment and labour disputes typically lies with the Labour Court. However, it's important to note that the specific jurisdiction can sometimes be determined by the nature of the dispute and the parties involved. For instance, certain disputes may be heard in the Federal High Court if they involve a sum of money exceeding N5 million (Nigerian Naira), as per the provisions of the Labour Act. Always check the latest legal updates and specific provisions as laws can change.
