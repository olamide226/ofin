Labour Act, Cap L1 LFN 2004, section 11(2): The notice to be given shall be- (a) one day, where the contract has continued for a period of three months or less; (b) one week, where the contract has continued for more than three months but less than two years; (c) two weeks, where the contract has continued for a period of two years but less than five years; and (d) one month, where the contract has continued for five years or more.
