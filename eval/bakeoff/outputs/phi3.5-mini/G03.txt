|-\|/-\ According to the Labour Act, Cap LFN 2004, section 54(1), as a woman who has been continuously employed by your company for a period of six months or more, you are entitled to the following maternity leave and pay benefits:

(a) You have the right to leave your work if you produce a medical certificate stating that your confinement will probably take place within six weeks.

(b) You shall not be permitted to work during the six weeks following your confinement.

(c) During this six-week absence, you shall be paid not less than fifty per cent of the wages you would have earned if you had not been absent.

(d) If you are nursing your child, you shall be allowed half an hour twice a day during your working hours for that purpose.

Please note that the specific entitlements may vary depending on the company's policies and any additional regulations that may apply. Always refer to your company's maternity leave policy and consult with HR for detailed information. [Labour Act, Cap L1 LFN 2004, s.54(1)]
