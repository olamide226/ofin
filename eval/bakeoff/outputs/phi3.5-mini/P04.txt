National Minimum Wage Act 2019 (as amended 2024), section 3(1): Every employer shall pay a national minimum wage of not less than N70,000.00 per month. Section 4(1): Section 3(1) does not apply to- (a) part-time, commission or piece-rate workers; (b) an establishment employing less than 25 persons; (c) seasonal agricultural workers; (d) crew of vessels/aircraft under merchant shipping or civil aviation laws.
