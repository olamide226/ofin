|-\|/- According to the Labour Act, Cap LFN 2004, section 5(1), employers are not permitted to make deductions from workers' wages for fines unless expressly permitted by this Act or any other law. Furthermore, section 5(7) states that the total amount of deductions from a worker's wages in any one month should not exceed one-third of the wages for that month. If half of your salary has been deducted for the fine, this exceeds the one-third limit set by the Labour Act. Therefore, based on the provided statute, the deduction of 20,000 naira for being late and resulting in half your salary being deducted is not permissible under Nigerian labor law. [Labour Act, Cap L1 LFN 2004, sections 5(1) and 5(7)].
