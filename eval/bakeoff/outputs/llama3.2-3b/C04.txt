-\ I'm not certain about the specific details of the Nigerian Labour Act regarding payment of wages in store vouchers or goods instead of money. However, I can tell you that under the Nigerian Labour Act, an employer is required to pay wages in a manner that is acceptable to the worker.

Section 57 of the Nigerian Labour Act, 2004, states that an employer shall pay wages due to an employee in cash or in a bank draft. 

I couldn't find any provision that explicitly allows an employer to pay wages in store vouchers or goods.
