Labour Act, Cap L1 LFN 2004, section 16: Subject to the Workmen's Compensation Act, a worker shall be entitled to be paid wages up to twelve working days in any one calendar year during absence from work caused by temporary illness certified by a registered medical practitioner.

QUESTION: Abeg explain this sick leave matter for Pidgin make everybody fit understand am.

|-\ Na so, wetin Labour Act dey say about sick leave? 

Section 16 of Labour Act, Cap L1 LFN 2004, say am. 

Am say, worker wey dey sick, na so worker go receive pay, but na for twelve working days wey worker dey absent for work. 

Na for calendar year, so na for one year wey worker dey count. 

Na for sickness wey worker dey suffer, na sickness wey worker dey get from registered medical practitioner wey dey certify sickness. 

Na so, worker go receive pay, but na for twelve working days wey worker dey absent for work, biko.
